About Us

lifecycle.in is a specialized consultancy focused on helping businesses navigate the complexities of Battery Waste Management under the Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR) regulations established in 2022.

We bridge the gap between regulatory compliance and sustainable waste management through innovative technology and strategic partnerships across India.

Our team combines expertise in environmental regulations, waste management, and technological solutions to provide comprehensive EPR services to battery producers and manufacturers.

Our Approach

Assessment

Our Technology

AI-Driven Waste Management

Our proprietary AI system optimizes the entire battery waste management lifecycle, from collection to recycling:

  • Automated segregation protocols based on battery chemistry and condition
  • Recycling pathway optimization to maximize material recovery
  • Second-life assessment algorithms to identify refurbishment candidates
  • Real-time tracking and reporting for complete transparency
